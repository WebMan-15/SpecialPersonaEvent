Astha Gill

Aviation Veteran, Educationist & Trainer
20 - years of Dedication & Hardwork

Hi! I am Astha – An Accomplished Trainer cum Educationist with 2 decades of professional experience. I am an Aviation veteran , started my professional journey at a very young age. I started flying as a Flight Attendant, many of you commonly know this job as- Airhostess /Cabin Crew.

I started working in my teenage. My Journey with the Aviation industry has been quite exciting …while I was enjoying flying as an Inflight Manager with the then Best Airline of India- Jet Airways…I also ventured out training opportunities within the Airline.

As a Trainer I was able to serve various domains and trained professionals from almost all departments of the Airline… including Pilots, Managers, Cabin Crew, ground personnel etc.

I attained training accreditation from 3 different international platforms and held even more responsible positions like Training manager and Performance manager during her tenure with Jet Airways.

The exposure of such varied domains gave me the Golden opportunity to work for Directorate General of Civil Aviation – the Indian Aviation regulatory body as a Cabin safety Inspector …so Inspection, audits , approvals is what became a part of my profile…

And presently – I happen to be the Only person empanelled as Cabin Safety Subject matter expert with Aircraft Accident Investigation Bureau of India…so that makes me a responsible Aviation Incident and Accident Investigator too.

I am justifying my entire professional journey by presently nurturing a beautiful Learning Gallery – famously known as Special Persona Learning Gallery.

I believe my present role is not just a Professional Venture…it has much more to it…let us talk in a personal session where I get a chance to share my exciting professional journey…and you share yours!

Because… Sharing is Caring!
